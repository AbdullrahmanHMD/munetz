Here we are showing two cases of people struggling with the current system. The first person who has a meeting to discuss the approval of a project and needs to know more about it. And the second person who is looking for the department responsible for a project that she's currently reading about to get more information and ask questions.

Axiona can function as a standalone website or an extension that has functionalities built on top of the existing website.

Its core functionality is providing a project's general information and summarizing its documents.

The two additional features that it has are answering detailed queries about a specific project or helping to find other projects in the system by providing the appropriate search prompt that can be pasted in the search bar of the current website.
